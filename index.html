HOW TO RECYCLE IN OTANIEMI

We do not want to be as annoying as geese, do we? Well, of course not, and that is why we are learning how to dump our shit in the right place.


Where to take my waste?


Everyday recycling in Teekkari Village is simple and does not require a lot of extra effort. Recycling bins for mixed waste, biowaste, metal, glass, magazines/paper and cardboard can be found very close to almost every student apartment building. So, recycling these items is fairly easy and we have even made a map of all the recycling areas in Teekkari Village for you! Check it out from the bottom of this page.

Also, we made a quick checklist for other waste that regularly occurs at home and requires a tiny bit of extra effort for us to recycle:

A) BATTERIES
All the Alepas accept used batteries, including Alepa Otaniemi.

B) TIN CANS, GLASS BOTTLES AND PLASTIC BOTTLES
Tin cans, glass bottles and plastic bottles can all be taken to grocery stores, including Alepa Otaniemi.

C) LIGHT BULBS
Light bulbs cannot be thrown to normal waste, since they require SER-recycling (SER-kierrÃ¤tys). Closest SER collection points can be found from https://www.kierratys.info. Old halogen lamps, incandescent lamps and car lamps can be thrown with normal waste.


Recycling during a move


During a move there is probably furniture and other items you want to get rid of. No furniture should be left in other parts of a shared flat, in the hallways or in the waste container areas. Instead, there are several way to recycle such items in Teekkari Village.

Likewise, when you need new stuff, keep in mind that instead of buying brand new items you often can get them for a very cheap price or free of charge from other Teekkari Villagers.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about recycling in Teekkari Village

Q: Where to recycle batteries?
A: Old batteries can be taken to Alepa or the AYY office.

Q: Where is the recycling center?
A: It is located in the basement of Servin Maijan Tie 6 B.

Q: Where to leave my furniture so that other people can take them?
A: Take your old furniture to the recycling center or put it up for grabs in groups such as Aalto Marketplace or Teekkari Village Market on Facebook. Do not leave any furniture at the normal trash containers!

Q: Where are all the recycling spots in Otaniemi?
A: Check out our recycling map to see the where the spots are located!
